Lasagna

Description

This recipe will be saucy and cheesy. P.S. don't tell your mom.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Sauce
  • Meat
  • Cheese

Steps

  • Boil the noodles.
  • Layer the noodles in a pan.
  • Alternate layering noodles, sauce, meat and cheese.
  • Top with more cheese and bake at 425 degrees for 25 minutes.
